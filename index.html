Brewing tea is the most relaxing step when it comes to making your perfect cup of tea. Make sure you have taken a peek at our page about the different varieties of tea, to pick your tea-type today. Tea leaves come in many forms, the most common being tea-bags and loose leaf tea. If you are using loose leaf tea, make sure to keep a strainer handy. Here's a list of equipment you require, which are easy to find around the home:

  • • Tea Leaves | either tea-bags or loose leaf
  • • Electric Kettle or Teapot | to boil your water in
  • • Filter or Strainer | a must-have for loose leaf tea, skip for tea-bags
  • • Cup or Mug | your favourite cup or mug
  • • Spoon | to stir the tea with
