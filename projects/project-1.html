The purpose of this project was to create an inforgraphic representing three trips from my student dorm to three different destinations: my class in Ryder Hall, my hometown, and to my boyfriend's house in Connecticut; using symbols and labels. I recorded all the different activities I did during each of the trips, and additionally, created a breakdown of the usage of my time during the longest one.
